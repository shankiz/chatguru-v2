1. Refund Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your AI Anime Voice experience. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Request is made within 7 days of purchase
  • Technical issues prevented service use
  • Service was not as described
  • Account has not exceeded reasonable usage limits

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Used message credits or consumed services
  • Character unlocks that have been used
  • Accounts banned for Terms of Service violations
  • Purchases older than 7 days

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Contact our support team at support@aianimevoice.com
  • Include your order number and reason for refund
  • Provide any relevant details about your experience

We will process your request within 2-3 business days.

4. Refund Method

Refunds will be processed through your original payment method. Processing times may vary:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Other payment methods: Up to 14 business days

5. Subscription Cancellations

For subscription-based services:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time
  • Access continues until the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds for unused subscription time
  • Renewal payments will be automatically stopped

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@aianimevoice.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
  • Support Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM (UTC+8)
